OPENSTREETMAP

About OSM

OpenStreetMap is the world's openly edited base map of the world. Its used by a variety of professionals around the world, and provides assistance in humanitarian and disaster relief situations. This Badge Pathway will help you understand OpenStreetMap as a project, and will get you started with an OSM account of your own and making your first edits.
